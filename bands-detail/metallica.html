Metallica

Metallica is an American heavy metal band formed in 1981 in Los Angeles, California. They are considered one of the "Big Four" of thrash metal and have sold over 125 million records worldwide.

Genre: Heavy Metal, Thrash Metal
Members: James Hetfield (vocals, rhythm guitar), Lars Ulrich (drums), Kirk Hammett (lead guitar), Robert Trujillo (bass)
Famous Albums: Kill 'Em All (1983), Master of Puppets (1986), Metallica (1991), Death Magnetic (2008), 72 Seasons (2023)
