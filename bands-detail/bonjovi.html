Bon Jovi

Bon Jovi is an American rock band formed in 1983 in Sayreville, New Jersey. Fronted by Jon Bon Jovi, the band is known for their anthemic rock sound and has sold over 130 million records worldwide.

Genre: Rock, hard rock
Members: Jon Bon Jovi (vocals), Richie Sambora (guitar), David Bryan (keyboards), Tico Torres (drums), Hugh McDonald (bass)
Famous Albums: Bon Jovi (1984), Slippery When Wet (1986), New Jersey (1988), Keep the Faith (1992), Crush (2000)
