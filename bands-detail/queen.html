Queen

Queen is a British rock band formed in London in 1970. Known for their theatrical style and operatic compositions, they have sold over 300 million records worldwide, making them one of the best-selling music artists ever.

Genre: Rock, progressive rock
Members: Freddie Mercury (vocals, piano, 1970–1991), Brian May (guitar, vocals), John Deacon (bass, 1971–1997), Roger Taylor (drums, vocals)
Famous Albums: A Night at the Opera (1975), The Game (1980), A Kind of Magic (1986), Innuendo (1991), Made in Heaven (1995)
