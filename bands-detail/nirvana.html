Nirvana

Nirvana was an American rock band formed in Aberdeen, Washington, in 1987. They were pivotal in bringing grunge music to a mainstream audience and are often credited with popularizing alternative rock.

Genre: Grunge, alternative rock
Members: Kurt Cobain (vocals, guitar, 1987–1994), Krist Novoselic (bass, 1987–1994), Dave Grohl (drums, 1990–1994)
Famous Albums: Bleach (1989), Nevermind (1991), In Utero (1993)
