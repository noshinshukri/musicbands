Depeche Mode

Depeche Mode is an English electronic music band formed in Basildon, Essex, in 1980. They are one of the most successful bands in the history of popular music, with over 100 million records sold worldwide.

Genre: Synth-pop, new wave, electronic
Members: Dave Gahan (vocals), Martin Gore (keyboards, guitar, vocals), Andy Fletcher (keyboards, 1980–2022)
Famous Albums: Violator (1990), Songs of Faith and Devotion (1993), Ultra (1997), Playing the Angel (2005), Spirit (2017)
