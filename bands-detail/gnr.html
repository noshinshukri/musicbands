Guns N' Roses

Guns N' Roses is an American hard rock band formed in Los Angeles in 1985. They have sold more than 100 million records worldwide, including 45 million in the United States.

Genre: Hard rock, heavy metal
Members: Axl Rose (vocals), Slash (guitar), Duff McKagan (bass), Dizzy Reed (keyboards), Richard Fortus (guitar), Frank Ferrer (drums), Melissa Reese (keyboards)
Famous Albums: Appetite for Destruction (1987), Use Your Illusion I & II (1991), Chinese Democracy (2008)
